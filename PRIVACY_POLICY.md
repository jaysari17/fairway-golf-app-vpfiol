
# Privacy Policy for FAIRWAY

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

FAIRWAY ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Profile Information:** Username, bio, handicap (optional)
- **Golf Round Data:** Course names, dates, ratings, reviews, scores, photos
- **Course Ratings:** Your preferences and ratings for golf courses

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App features used, interaction patterns
- **Crash Reports:** Technical data when the app crashes (if you opt-in)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the FAIRWAY app functionality
- Store your golf rounds and course ratings locally on your device
- Calculate statistics and achievements
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage

**Local Storage:** All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers.

**No Cloud Sync:** Currently, FAIRWAY does not sync your data to the cloud. Your data remains on your device.

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Transmit your golf data to external servers
- Track you across other apps or websites

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data stored in the app
- Delete your data by uninstalling the app
- Export your data (feature coming soon)
- Opt-out of crash reporting

## Children's Privacy

FAIRWAY is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:
- Local encrypted storage
- Secure coding practices
- Regular security updates

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- **Email:** support@fairway.app
- **Website:** https://fairway.app

## Third-Party Services

FAIRWAY uses the following third-party services:
- **Expo:** Development framework (https://expo.dev/privacy)
- **React Native:** Mobile framework (https://reactnative.dev)

These services may collect anonymous usage data to improve their platforms.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:
- Delete specific rounds or ratings
- Clear app data
- Uninstall the application

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)

## European Privacy Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

By using FAIRWAY, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
